Introduction to Skills

In todays world people always say "you should invest in yourself" and you may wonder well what does this mean. Investing in yourself might mean developing your knowledge and increasing your personal skillset. On this page I have discovered the most useful books, websites, and useful skills to learn in order to create the life you want.

Educating yourself

There is a saying that goes knowledge is power, this is because it gives you the ability like reasoning and problem solving. It is important to continually educate yourself to broaden your capabilites, increase career opportunities, make more informed decisions, will allow you to uphold meaningful conversations and the knowledge you gain will be with you forever.

Earn with your mind, not with your time

Working hard is a great skill to develop, but if your trying to achieve a high income working more hours or picking up a second job is'nt always the best solution. Developing high income skills by teaching yourself or attending university will allow you to earn more for for the same hours worked. Sometimes learning new skills can be challenging when you may already have a full time job but there are alternatives such as online courses, youtube videos, websites and part time university.

Useful careers for the future

In todays world artificial intelligence and cutting-edge technology is becoming more advanced which is slowly phasing out tasks that could otherwise be replaced by machinery. Some useful careers that will be beneficial for the future could inlcude software developers, enegineers, scientists, data analysts, information systems, healthcare professionals, business professionals, constuction workers, and creative careers. It is really important to consider what industires and job positions might be replaced by artificial intelligence in order to be needed in the job marketplace.
