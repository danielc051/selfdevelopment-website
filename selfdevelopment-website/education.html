Introduction to education

In todays world people always say "you should invest in yourself" and you may wonder well what does this mean. Investing in yourself might mean developing your knowledge and increasing your personal skillset. On this page I have discovered the most useful books, websites, and useful skills to learn in order to create the life you want.

Education alone isn't enough

Developing your skillset and increasing your knowledge is highly important but ultimately taking action towards your goal is the only way to get where you want.

Getting uncomfortable

Trying to learn new skills can be challenging. Everyone has to start somewhere so by setting goals, staying consistent and building up self discipline this will help you stay motived when your only motivator is you.

How to educate yourself

In order to educate yourself and develop your knowledge you will need to obtain information from sources which could include books, courses, podcasts, video content, online resources and learning by doing. Athough free content can be beneficial, purchasing a course or book and putting some skin in the game will force you to complete it. This is because you will simply wamt tp gain value from the money you spent.
