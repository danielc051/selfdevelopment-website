What is balanced living?

Balanced living essentially means optimally sustaining all aspects of your life. Areas could inlcude, relationships, work, fitness, health, and emotional happiness.

However, without trying to support all areas of your life it could be detremental to our future selves. For example, entrepenuers may neglect there health and relationships when trying to grow a business which might later cause them grief down the line. Ultimately, trying to keep a balance in all areas will allow you to living a more desirabale lifestyle.

How to eat effectively

When eating with the intention of a balanced lifestyle you are aiming to eat a wide variety of foods in the right proportions, and consuming the right amount of food to achieve and maintain a healthy body weight.

Perfect plate model

In order to ensure portion control when eating, nutritionists use the perfect plate model to recommend portion sizes which aims for the right proportions of each food group in every meal. This model fllows 1/2 plate non-startchy colourful vegetables e.g. lettuce, carrot, tomatoes, 1/4 plate carbohydrates e.g.potatoes, rice, kumara, and 1/4 plate protein e.g. meat, fish, chicken.
